e.l.f. Cosmetics

I joined e.l.f. when it was a lean start-up, just before its growth surge. This is where I cut my teeth on all things digital, and really fell in love with product design. I was relied upon to create the most efficient digital footprint—from the brand site, to email, and social. I art directed photo shoots, wireframed and launched site extensions, full site relaunches, and built a creative team. With a direct line to our founder and engineers, we worked fast and iterated often. This is an overview of the launch to responsive, with mobile as the main target.
